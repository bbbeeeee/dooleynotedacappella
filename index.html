Who are we?

Dooley Noted is an a cappella group based in Emory University. At the beginning of every school year we audition for new members that we then affectionately call newbies. The group is completely student run with an executive board that makes sure the group keeps true to its mission.

At the beginning of every semester, members have the opportunity to recommend songs that the arrangers of the group then spend time arranging to be better suited for a cappella music.

We pride ourselves in our mission to sing to those that need a break from their stressful lives and try to lighten their spirits as best we can!
